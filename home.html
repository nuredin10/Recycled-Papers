D.A Packaging PLC is a Paper & Carton manufacturing company that was established in 2002 G.C. with the purpose of manufacturing and supplying Corrugated Carton Boxes to a variety of industries. Since it’s birth, it extended its production capacity and products variety, and is now supplying others in the same industry with recycled paper as well as water-based ink, on top of fulfilling its own major raw material requirements.

Our New Venture

Desta Women’s Hospital

Desta Women's Hospital is a New Project by D.A Packaging P.L.C which is under construction located in the area commonly known as Mekanissa . This Hospital Project is 80 beds Women with a goal to provide compassionate and effective treatment to underserved women in Addis Ababa and the Surrounding Area.

The facility will have a Comprehensive Multi Specialty service exclusively catering to women and adolescents with a full-fledged Laboratory, Imaging, Ambulatory, and Emergency Service.
The opening date is expected in Mid 20223.
